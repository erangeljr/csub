Notes:

se: SignExtImm = 16 copies of sign bit [15] + imm16
ze: ZeroExtImm = zeros16 + imm16

Branch Address = 14 copies of sign bit + addr16 + zeros2 
Jump Address   = Copy of top 4 bits of PC + jumpAddr26 +  zeros2
