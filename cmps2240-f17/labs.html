CMPS-2240 Laboratory Assignments

Laboratory Assignments are designed to give you a quick hands-on experience, while receiving feedback and help from the instructor and teaching assistants.

Program Naming
Each lab assignment will designate a filename to use.

For example, Lab-1 program will be named lab1.s No caps.

Program Comments
Every program you write for me will have a comment section at the top, similar to the example below. 


# Program: Lab 1 part 1
# Author: Your Name
