CMPS-224 Homework Format

Homework may be done on paper, with pen or pencil, and handed in on Friday before the quiz begins.

If pen is used, please do not cross-out or scribble over any of your answers. Score will be 0. Please use a pencil and an eraser to correct your errors.

Homework may be sent in the body of an email.

If email is used, please keep lines of text under 80 characters per line.
